Who I am

I have been working in the Tech Industry since May 2018 after i decided to cross train from an amazing career in Logistics Management in the Royal Navy. I served in the military for nearly 10yrs. I decided I wanted to take a risk and learn start again in an industry I have always been passionate about.

I believe the fact that I decided to invest in my personal development helped me secure employment in this industry. If you are willing to learn then this will not be difficult for you. There are so many online resources you can use, talk to your superiors, get yourself a mentor or sponsor. At times you will have to make sacrifices to study but if you are doing something that you really enjoy then it’s not really a sacrifice. I use the 100days of code challenge to learn something for myself and am motivated to keep going even on the “bad code days” when I am getting errors and I don’t know why.
